AI travel command center for modern trip planning

Wayline turns scattered confirmations, booking tools, maps, calendars, documents, and spend into one persistent itinerary workspace for consumers and teams.

Problem Travel planning is fragmented

Travelers jump between email, booking sites, maps, calendars, notes, wallets, and spreadsheets. Trip context gets lost exactly when plans change.

Solution One itinerary operating system

Wayline centralizes confirmations, enriches them with AI extraction, connects external booking workflows, and keeps every trip searchable.

Business model Consumer freemium plus partner revenue

Premium sync, shared trips, business travel workspaces, affiliate booking handoffs, and API access for travel operators.

Moat Personal travel graph

Persistent trip data, supplier parsing, user preferences, document context, and calendar behavior compound into better automation over time.
